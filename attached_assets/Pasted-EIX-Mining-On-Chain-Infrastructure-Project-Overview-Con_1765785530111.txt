EIX Mining & On-Chain Infrastructure

Project Overview & Contributor / Partner Hiring Document


---

1. Core System Description

EIX is a mining-based blockchain ecosystem integrated with on-chain user verification and multi-chain no-code blockchain tools.
The system is designed to generate sustainable, recurring revenue while supporting long-term liquidity, listings, and ecosystem growth.

Revenue is generated through:

On-chain user verification fees

Token deployment and blockchain utility tools

App-based mining, staking, and harvesting mechanisms


A predefined percentage of all revenues is automatically reserved for liquidity, listings, and ecosystem sustainability.


---

2. On-Chain Verification Revenue Model

Verification fee per user: 0.01 ETH (approximately USD 30)

Each transaction covers blockchain and execution costs

30% to 50% of the fee is automatically reserved by the platform


Adoption Projection

Target users: 100,000

Total collected: 1,000 ETH (~USD 3,000,000)


System Reserve from Verification

30% reserve: ~USD 900,000

50% reserve: ~USD 1,500,000


These reserves are allocated to liquidity provisioning, exchange listings, team revenue sharing, and long-term ecosystem stability.
